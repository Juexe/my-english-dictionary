你有什么特别的事情想谈的吗？ above all /əˌbʌv ˈɔːl/ [adverb] use above all to emphasize that something is more important than all the other things you have mentioned 最重要的是 Get plenty of sleep, eat lots of good food, and above all try to relax. 要有充足的睡眠，多吃有营养的食物，最重要的是要尽量放松。 John felt sad, embarrassed, but above all angry that Anna could treat him like this. 约翰感到又伤心又难堪，但是最气愤的还是安娜这样待他。 most of all /ˌməʊst əv ˈɔːl/ [adverb] more than anything or anyone else 最 Swimming and soccer are fun, but I like dancing most of all. 游泳和足球都很有意思，但我最喜欢跳舞。 He was friendly and intelligent, but most of all he was a good worker. 他友善、聪明，但最重要的是他工作干得好。 Out of everybody at school she was the person who helped me most of all. 学校里所有的人当中，她给我的帮助最多。 least of all /ˌliːst əv ˈɔːl/ [adverb] especially not 尤其不，最不 She told no one, least of all her husband, what she planned to do. 她没有告诉任何人，更没有告诉她丈夫自己的打算。 Nobody wants to stop you from following the career of your choice, least of all me. 没有人想阻止你去追求自己所选择的事业，我更是不会。 notably /ˈnəʊtəbli/ [adverb] formal use this to say that someone or something is an important example of what you are talking about 【正式】尤其是，特别是〔用于举出重要的例子〕 The use of illegal drugs - notably marijuana - has increased in recent years. 非法毒品的使用—尤其是大麻—近年来有所增加。most notably A number of respected philosophers, most notably Leibniz, criticized Newton's theories. 一些受人尊敬的哲学家，尤其是莱布尼茨，批评了牛顿的理论。 of all people /əv ˈɔːl ˌpiːpəl/ [adverb] spoken more than anyone else - use this when someone has said or done something you think is very surprising or unlikely for them to do or say 【口】在所有人中偏偏，尤其〔用于表示某人的言语或行为令人吃惊〕 You of all people shouldn't be calling him worthless. 尤其是你最不应该说他无能。 Why is Jennifer Stern, of all people, so important?
