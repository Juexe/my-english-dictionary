say something in a loud, high voice, because you are excited, frightened, angry, or in pain 〔因激动、害怕、愤怒或疼痛而〕尖叫；尖声说 Everyone panicked, and people started screaming. 大家都惊慌了，人们开始尖叫起来。 ‘Go away!’ she 她惊恐得尖叫着醒了过来。 shriek /ʃriːk/ [intransitive/transitive verb] to make a sudden short screaming sound or shout something because you are very frightened, excited etc 尖叫，尖声喊叫 Bella turned and shrieked his because you are excited, amused, frightened, or in pain 〔因激动、开心、受惊或疼痛〕突然地尖叫 The children squealed and fought among themselves. 孩子们尖叫着互相打起来。 ‘Don't you dare!’ Bobby squealed. “你敢！”博比尖叫道。squeal with
