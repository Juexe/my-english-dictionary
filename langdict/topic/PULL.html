所有人都拿起绳子用力拉。 pull [countable noun] give something a pull She gave a gentle pull on the reins, and the horse stopped. 她轻轻一拉缰绳，那匹马便停住了。 That door sticks a bit - give it a good pull. 那扇门有点卡住——用力拉一下。 tug
