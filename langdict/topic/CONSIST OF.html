熔岩的化学成分是什么？ comprise /kəmˈpraɪz/ [transitive verb not in progressive] formal to consist of a number of parts, groups, organizations etc 【正式】包括；由…组成 The house comprises 2 bedrooms, a kitchen, and a living room. 这幢房子包括两间卧室、一个厨房和一间起居室。 The city's population comprises mainly Asians and Europeans. 该城人口主要由亚洲人和欧洲人组成。2 to have or include something as a part 包括某物作为一部分 have something in it /ˌhæv something ˈɪn ɪt/ [verb phrase] Does this fish have bones in it?
