in place of /ɪn ˈpleɪs ɒv/ [preposition] if one thing is used in place of another, it is used instead of it or put in the place where the other thing was 代替，取代 A Walt Disney film is being shown in place of the advertised programme. 现在放映一部沃尔特·迪斯尼的电影来代替事先预告的那个节目。 For this recipe you can always use olive oil in place of butter. 这个菜谱中，你总可以用橄榄油来代替黄油。in something's place The newspaper has stopped having a crossword puzzle and in its place they now have a weekly wordsearch competition. 这家报纸现已不刊登纵横字谜游戏了，取而代之的是每周一期的寻字比赛。 in favour of British /in favor of American /ɪn ˈfeɪvər ɒv/ [preposition] if you decide not to do, have, or accept one thing in favour of another, you choose the other because you think it is much better 支持；赞同 The original plan was abandoned in favour of a new proposal. 原计划被放弃，采纳了新的建议。 Communist theory and practice has been rejected in favour of American-style capitalism. 福利主义已被抛弃，实行了美国式的资本主义。 in preference to /ɪn ˈprefərəns tuː/ [preposition] if you choose one thing something that is given in lieu of something else is given instead of that thing, especially in a work or business situation 代替〔尤指与工作或商业有关的情况〕 a day/time off in lieu Because you worked on Sunday, you can have a day off in lieu. 由于周日上了班，你可以休假一天。 Occasionally, he makes his staff work overtime and gives them time off in lieu. 他偶尔让员工加班，再给他们补假。in lieu of They used to give the landlord poultry and eggs 下周的比赛我不参加了，我不清楚谁来替我。in place of somebody A new manager was appointed in place of Hoddle. 新任命了一位经理接替霍德尔。 for /fəʳ; strong fɔːʳ/ [preposition] if you do something for someone, you do it instead of them,
