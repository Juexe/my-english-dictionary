你的戒指是特别订做的吗？ particular /pəʳˈtɪkjɑləʳ/ [adjective only before noun] use this to emphasize that something is different or separate from other things of the same kind, or that something is more important than other things 特别的，特殊的〔用于强调某事物与众不同或更重要〕 Is there any particular reason why you want to go back to Japan?
