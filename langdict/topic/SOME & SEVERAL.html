SOME/SEVERAL1 some, but not a large number2 some, but not a large amount3 some, but not allRELATED WORDSsee alsoFEW/NOT MANYLOT1 some, but not a large number 一些，但数目不多的 some /səm, strong sʌm/ [determiner/pronoun] a number of people or things, but not a large number - use this when you are not saying exactly what the number is 一些，若干 There were some children playing in the street. 街上有些孩子在玩。 ‘Have we got any biscuits?’ ‘Yes, I've just bought some.’ “我们有饼干吗？”“有啊，我刚刚买了一些。”some of Come over here and meet some of my friends. 过来吧，认识一下我的一些朋友。some more I've got some more photos of her upstairs. 我楼上还有一些她的照片。some other There are some other reasons as well as those I have mentioned. 除了我提到的那些，还有其他一些原因。 a few /ə ˈfjuː/ [quantifier] a small number of people, things etc 少数，几个，一些 ‘Are there any chocolates left?’ ‘Only a few.’ “还有巧克力吗？”“只有几块了。” They went to China a few years ago. 他们几年前去过中国。 We know a few people who work in advertising. 我们认识一些从事广告工作的人。a few of I'm going to the club with a few of my friends. 我要和几个朋友一起去俱乐部。 several /ˈsevərəl/ [quantifier] more than a few people or things, but not a large number 几个，数个 The President visited several states on his tour. 总统这次出行访问了几个州。 We've had several meetings, but nothing has been agreed. 我们开了几次会，但什么都没定下来。 She's been to Japan several times. 她去过几次日本。several of I've read several of his books and they're really good. 我读过他的几本书，写得非常好。 Several of the islands have beautiful beaches. 有几个岛上海滩很美。 a number of /ə ˈnʌmbər ɒv/ [quantifier] several 几个，一些 A number of people said they had seen the stolen van earlier in the day. 好几个人说这天早些时候看到过被盗的小型货车。 We have received a number of complaints about last night's programme. 我们接到好几个有关昨晚节目的投诉。 A number of her ideas were very good indeed. 她的一些想法确实非常好。 a couple of /ə ˈkʌpəl ɒv/ [quantifier] informal a very small number but at least two 【非正式】几个〔最少两个〕 I'd just like to ask you a couple of questions before you go. 你走之前我想问你几个问题。 Those baskets are nice, and they only cost a couple of quid. 那些篮子很不错，而且只要几英镑。 There wasn't much mail this morning - just a couple of bills. 今天早上邮件不多，只有几张账单。2 some, but not a large amount 一些，但数量不多的 some /səm, strong sʌm/ [determiner/pronoun] an amount of something, but not a large amount - use this when you are not saying exactly what the amount is 一些，若干 Can I borrow some money, Dad? 我可以借些钱吗，爸爸？ I need some time to think about what you've said. 你说的事，我需要一点时间想想。 ‘We've run out of milk.’ ‘Do you want me to go and get some?’ “我们没有牛奶了。”“要我去买一些吗？”some of not the whole thing 一点，一些 Have some of this cake -- it's delicious. 吃点这蛋糕吧，味道很好。some more Would you like some more wine? 你要再来点葡萄酒吗？ a certain amount of /ə ˌsɜːʳtn əˈmaʊnt ɒv/ [quantifier] a fairly large amount - use this to talk about people's feelings, abilities etc 一定数量的，相当多的〔指人的情感、能力等〕 It's a job that requires a certain amount of intelligence and skill. 这份工作需要一定的聪明才智和技术。 A certain amount of stress is unavoidable in daily life. 日常生活当中有一定的压力是不可避免的。 a measure of /ə ˈmeʒər ɒv/ [quantifier] formal a fairly large amount of something, especially something good 【正式】一定程度的〔尤指好的事物〕 There is a measure of flexibility in the system. 这种制度有一定的灵活性。 Having a job gives me a measure of independence. 有一份工作可给我一定的独立性。3 some, but not all 部分 some /sʌm/ [quantifier] some but not all of a number or amount 〔某个数量中的〕部分，一些 Some trees lose their leaves in the autumn. 有些树木秋天会落叶。 Some students only come here because they want to have fun, not because they want to learn. 有些学生来这里只是因为想玩，而不是想学习。some of not all the people or things in a group 一些，部分 ‘Have you met Jack's friends?’ ‘Some of them.’ “你见过杰克的朋友吗？”“见过一些。” I've only spent some of the money. 我只花了一部分钱。
